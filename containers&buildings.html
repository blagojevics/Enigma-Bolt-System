Containers

Containers can have different application. They consist of galvanized steel construction which provides: possibility of joining vertically and horizontally, forklift and crane manipulation. The insulation is made of durable sandwich pannels. Carpentry Al or PVC. Dimensions WxLxH: 2,44 x 5,98 x 2,62.

Buildings

By stacking multiple modules, space is obtained as desired. They can be one-storey, two-storey and three-storey. Camps, apartments, schools, hospitals, military objects… The interior can be equipped in the same way as in standard construction.
